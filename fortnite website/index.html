FORTNITE

  • What is FORTNITE
    Fortnite is a third person shooter survival game where the player has to survive against up to 100 other players. The total player count for each battle royale is 100. The player can build forts through collecting materials such as metal, bricks, wood, and collect weapons before fighting other players.
  • Where can we play FORTNITE
    Fortnite is now available worldwide on Android, and on iOS and iPadOS in the European Union, thanks to the DMA. Get it through the mobile Epic Games Store
  • Types of game modes in Fortnite
    Fortnite offers a dynamic suite of six game modes, each built on the same robust engine and core mechanics but offering distinct experiences.They are : Fortnite Battle royale,creative,lego Fortnite,rocket racing and Fortnite festival
